  <!-- Section 1: The Enclosure -->
  <section style="margin-bottom: 30px;">
    <h3 style="background: #000; color: #fff; padding: 8px 12px; font-size: 1rem; display: inline-block;">1. THE ENCLOSURE (ACOUSTIC MASS)</h3>
    <div style="margin-top: 15px; border-left: 2px solid #eee; padding-left: 20px;">
      <p>Commercial speakers rely on thin, resonant plastic injection molding. The Sovereign Node mandates <strong>15mm Baltic Birch Plywood</strong> for its superior strength-to-weight ratio and internal damping.</p>
      <ul style="list-style: none; padding: 0;">
        <li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Internal Bracing:</strong> Install a "Window Brace" or cross-brace connecting the side panels to eliminate "panel talk" (harmonic vibration of the wood itself).</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Airtight Sealing:</strong> Use wood glue on all joints and internal silicone caulking. The cabinet must be a pressure vessel.</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Gasketing:</strong> Apply closed-cell foam gaskets to the driver mounts. Any air leak destroys the compression required for the 48Hz bloom.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Section 2: Porting Logic -->
  <section style="margin-bottom: 30px;">
    <h3 style="background: #000; color: #fff; padding: 8px 12px; font-size: 1rem; display: inline-block;">2. THE PORTING LOGIC (MECHANICAL SUMMING)</h3>
    <div style="margin-top: 15px; border-left: 2px solid #eee; padding-left: 20px;">
      <p>The port is not just a hole; it is a mechanical resonator.</p>
      <ul style="list-style: none; padding: 0;">
        <li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Tuning Frequency:</strong> Tune the physical cabinet to <strong>45Hz</strong>.</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>The Summing Effect:</strong> Tuning the port to 45Hz allows the mechanical resonance of the air to work in harmony with the <strong>48Hz hardware bloom</strong> in the circuit.</li>
        <li><strong>Result:</strong> This creates a 3Hz "spread" that produces massive, haptic bass depth that feels physical rather than processed.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </section>
