Enclosure Design (Structural Control) </h3> <p> The enclosure is constructed from <strong>15mm Baltic Birch plywood</strong>, selected for stiffness, density, and predictable resonance behavior in high-SPL applications. </p> <ul> <li><strong>Internal Bracing:</strong> Cross-bracing required to reduce panel vibration modes and cabinet coloration.</li> <li><strong>Airtight Construction:</strong> All joints sealed to prevent air leakage affecting low-frequency response.</li> <li><strong>Driver Gasket Isolation:</strong> Closed-cell foam used to decouple driver frame from cabinet.</li> </ul> </section> <section style="margin-bottom:20px;"> <h3 style="background:#000;color:#fff;padding:6px 10px;display:inline-block;font-size:0.95rem;"> 2. Port Tuning (Low-Frequency Alignment) </h3> <p> The enclosure is tuned as a bass reflex system with a target resonance near <strong>45 Hz</strong>. </p> <ul> <li><strong>Purpose:</strong> Extend low-frequency response and reduce driver excursion below tuning frequency.</li> <li><strong>Design Note:</strong> Port tuning must be validated through measurement (impedance sweep / frequency response).</li> </ul> </section> <section style="margin-bottom:20px;"> <h3 style="background:#b00020;color:#fff;padding:6px 10px;display:inline-block;font-size:0.95rem;"> 3. Safety &amp; Operation</h3> <ul> <li>High SPL levels may cause hearing damage — hearing protection recommended during calibration.</li> <li>Amplifier thermal management must be ensured under sustained high output conditions.</li> <li>Electrical system operates at elevated voltage levels (36V rail architecture).</li> </ul> </section> <div style="margin-top:25px; text-align:center; font-size:0.75rem; color:#777;"> SOVEREIGN THROW NODE // FILE: docs/BUILD_GUIDE.md </div> </div>
