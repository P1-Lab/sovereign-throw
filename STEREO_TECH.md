    <h3 style="background: #000; color: #fff; padding: 8px 12px; font-size: 1rem; display: inline-block;">2. DUAL-NODE WIRING LOGIC</h3>
    <div style="margin-top: 15px; border-left: 2px solid #eee; padding-left: 20px;">
      <p>To maintain signal integrity across two high-power units, follow the <strong>Forensic Balanced</strong> protocol:</p>
      <ul style="list-style: none; padding: 0;">
        <li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Source Connection:</strong> Use a Left/Right Balanced XLR pair from the mixer or preamp. Each Node receives its own dedicated discrete signal.</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Power Isolation:</strong> Each Node must maintain its own 10S2P battery pack and internal 36V rails. Do not "daisy-chain" DC power between cabinets.</li>
        <li style="margin-bottom: 12px;"><strong>Grounding:</strong> Maintain the Star-Ground logic within each cabinet. The signal grounds are isolated via the balanced XLR inputs to prevent ground loops across the stereo field.</li>
      </ul>
