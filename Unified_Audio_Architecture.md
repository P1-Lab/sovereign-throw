1. System Overview
</h3>

<p>
The Provision1 Acoustic Platform is a unified audio engineering system spanning three physical product categories:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Personal monitoring (headphones)</li>
<li>Near-field monitoring (desktop reference system)</li>
<li>Medium-field / portable reinforcement (PA system)</li>
</ul>

<p>
The platform is defined by a shared design philosophy emphasizing:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Controlled acoustic behavior across enclosure scales</li>
<li>Low-latency signal integrity</li>
<li>Mechanically stable enclosure systems</li>
<li>Consistent tonal and transient response across form factors</li>
</ul>
